About the game

Tickets that hate you back

Locked in a limbo with nothing but cursed tickets, you scratch through escalating decks to keep a bloodthirsty streak counter from hitting zero.

  • Each ticket is a ruleset: match icons, dodge skulls, obey weird conditions or get shredded.
  • Push your luck by stacking wins; cash out early or let greed flip the table.
  • Meta‑progression unlocks new ticket types, modifiers and truly awful strategies.
